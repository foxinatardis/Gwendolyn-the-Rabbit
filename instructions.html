Controls:

  • LEFT Arrow Key: Move Left
  • RIGHT Arrow Key: Move Right
  • UP Arrow Key: Jump / Double Jump
  • DOWN Arrow Key while on ground: Eat Carrots
  • DOWN Arrow Key while jumping: Stomp
  • SPACEBAR: Pause / Unpause Game
  • ENTER: Start new game

Gameplay:

  • Eat a carrot to gain 100 points and speed up the game.
  • Stomp a snowman to gain 150 points and slow down the game slightly.
  • Collect snowballs to gain 10 points each.
  • Reach a score of 5000 points to gain your first "free life"
  • Every time you earn a "free life" the score you need for your next life doubles.

Currently it is recommended to play "Gwendolyn the Rabbit" in Chrome or Safari. Additional browser support slated for future development.
